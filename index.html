Welcome

Saigon Thai, located at the heart of Downtown Moncton, has served a local favourite for 15+ years.

We serve freshly prepared, authentic dishes with distinctive flavours, featuring our most beloved dish Thai Curry Shrimp and Pad Thai. As all dishes are prepared in the kitchen, we are able to cater for most dietary needs, with over 90% of our menu all created gluten free. Saigon Thai also offers a large selection of Vegetarian dishes.

Fully licensed establishment, we have imported beers and a wide selection of wines.
